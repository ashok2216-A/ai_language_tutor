How LinguaAI Works

  • Evaluates speaking, writing, reading, and listening skills

AI-Powered

Our assessment uses advanced natural language processing to analyze your responses with the same accuracy as human examiners.

Follow Your Personalized Plan

Based on your diagnostic results, our AI creates a tailored learning path designed to maximize your improvement.

Our algorithm continuously adjusts your learning path based on your performance, ensuring you're always challenged but never overwhelmed.

Engage with our interactive modules designed to build your skills through regular practice and detailed feedback.

Real-time Feedback

Get instant, actionable feedback on your performance, allowing you to make corrections and improve faster than traditional methods.

Monitor your improvement with comprehensive analytics that show your growth over time.

Data-Driven
